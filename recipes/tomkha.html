Tom Kha

Tom kha is a creamy soup originating from Thailand. The most notable flavors are the lemongrass, galangal, and the kaffir lime leaves. The creaminess is from the coconut milk that we add. This soup is a milder version of Tom yum. In this recipe we will be using chicken, but you can use any other protein that you would like. In Thailand, they love adding in fresh seafood!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 cups Chicken broth or stock, unsalted
  • 1 1/2 cups Coconut milk
  • 1 stalk Lemongrass
  • 12 thing slices of galangal
  • 5 Kaffir lime leaves
  • 3-4 Thai Chillies
  • 5.5 oz of Oyster mushrooms
  • 2 Tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 tsp Palm sugar
  • Cilantro or green onions for garnish
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 tsp of kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring chicken stock and salt to a boil, add in the chicken
  2. Simmer the chicken for about 15 minutes or until fork tender
  3. Add in coconut milk
  4. Add the herbs and simmer for another 5 minutes
  5. Add the fish sauce and sugar and bring back to a boil
  6. Add the oyster mushrooms and cook for a minute or so
  7. Turn off the heat, add the lime juice
  8. Garnish with some cilantro or green onions and it is ready to serve!
